#STACK:
follows LIFO(LAST IN FIRST OUT)
insertion-push
deletion-pop
in stack,when the given size is exceeded--overflow
in stack,when the given list or array is empty--underflow


#QUEUE:
Follows FIFO(FIRST IN FIRST OUT)
1.Enqueue-insertion
2.Dequeue-deletion
insertion(add)-put
deletion(pop)-get
